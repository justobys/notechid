Privacy Policy for AutoPostImage

Effective Date: June 23, 2025

Welcome to AutoPostImage. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it.

1. Information We Collect

Our application, AutoPostImage, is an automation tool designed to help users post content to their Facebook Pages. We DO NOT collect, store, or share any personal information from our users. The application does not request personal data such as your name, email, or friend list.

2. How We Use Information

The only interaction our application has with your account is through the Facebook API to perform the following actions:

  • Obtain a Page Access Token with your permission.
  • Use that token to publish content (images and text) to the Facebook Page you manage.

We do not use your data for any other purpose, such as advertising or analytics.

3. Data Deletion

Since we do not store your personal data, there is no data to be deleted from our servers. If you wish to revoke the application's access to your Facebook account, you can do so at any time through your Facebook Account Settings under "Apps and Websites".

4. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.

5. Contact Us
